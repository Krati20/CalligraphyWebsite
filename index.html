About Us

Intro

It’s our joy to foster inspiration through the art of pointed-pen calligraphy. With more than 20 years of calligraphy experience, We have been serving our clients with our wide-range of handwritten scripts as well as stationery design.

Roots of InkSpell

Ink_Spell Calligraphy has been serving up handwritten scripts since 2002 for countless couples, corporate clients, and even a celeb or two (yes, stars: they love calligraphy just like us!), handcrafting everything from addressed envelopes to stationery design & print production to custom signage, all from our Old Town Alexandria, Virginia studio.
